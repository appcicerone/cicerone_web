Delete Your Account

You can request permanent deletion of your Cicerone account and all associated data at any time.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  1. Your account credentials (username and password)
  2. All saved and past itineraries
  3. Your travel preferences and interests
  4. Any other personal data associated with your account

How to request deletion

Send an email to us from the address associated with your Cicerone account:

info@ciceronetravel.app

Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the mail used for registration. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 business days.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you later wish to use Cicerone again, you will need to create a new account.

What we retain

After deletion, we may retain anonymized and aggregated data derived from your preferences and trips (e.g., popular destinations, travel styles). This data cannot be linked back to you and is used solely to improve our recommendation engine.
