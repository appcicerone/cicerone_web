FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Cicerone?

Cicerone is a travel planning app that uses artificial intelligence to create personalized day-by-day itineraries, tailored to your tastes and the time you have available.

How does Cicerone generate my itinerary?

You choose your destination and dates, answer a short questionnaire about your preferences, and our AI generates a full plan with places, times and routes for each day.

What languages is it available in?

Cicerone is available in English, Spanish, French, German and Italian.

When will the app be available?

We're in the final stretch of development. You'll soon be able to download it on the App Store and Google Play.
